6Risk Assessment (Preliminary)

6.1 Identified Risks
Site and Environmental Risks
  • ▸Monsoon-related flooding and dewatering risk during basement excavation.
  • ▸Urban traffic, dust, and noise impacts in a busy commercial corridor.
Health and Safety Risks
  • ▸Deep excavation and basement works create collapse, access, and confined-space hazards.
  • ▸Working at height and lifting operations during superstructure construction.
Schedule Risks
  • ▸Delay in basement completion before monsoon could impact the entire critical path.
  • ▸Approval delays or long-lead MEP/façade procurement may compress the finishing period.
Cost and Resource Risks
  • ▸Steel and concrete price fluctuations may affect the structural package cost.
  • ▸Skilled labour availability may tighten during peak overlapping phases.

Summary of Recommended Workforce and Resources
The TechNova IT Park project is a high-complexity commercial office development in a strong urban location on Avinashi Road, Coimbatore. The site is suitable for the proposed scale, with the main execution priority being timely completion of both basement levels before peak monsoon. A peak workforce of about 420 and average workforce of about 285 are recommended, supported by locally sourced Fe500D TMT steel and RMC procurement.
